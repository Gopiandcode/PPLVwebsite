History of the Area

To provide some context to the importance of PPLV, we've included a very brief overview of the history of PPLV, from the Birth of Computational Analysis, to the creation of Separation Logic.

Note: As PPLV is heavily focused on Logic in Computer Science - a history of PPLV would be incomplete without any mention of the history of Logic - thus, for the sake of completeness, we've also included important events in the history of Logic.

Let's get started...

The Birth of Logic

500 B.C

Sophists in Greece develop sets of rules to quantatively determine who will win an argument - this is the earliest documented record of anything resembling formal logic.

Advancements in Mathematical Logic

19th Century to 20th Century
After establishing logic as a method of verifying mathematical proofs, maths research became more rigorous and questions about whether all theorems could be proven began to arise. Leading to....
Godel's Incompleteness Theorem which prooved definitely using logic that not all mathematical theorems could be proven.

Closing words

The concept of Logic is inherently present in all technological advancements. Since the foundation of traditional logic by Aristotle, where every idea had to be either True or False, Logic has been increasingly embedded in the realm of mathematics and ultimately has become integral to the foundations of advanced technology. Hence, a field such as Principles of Programming, Logic and Verification which is focused on the development of Logic is going to play a role of increasing importance in the modern world, as our technologies become more and more complex.
For example, considering the ever-increasing amount of information available, scientists and engineers are constantly seeking to improve the methods of processing data to keep up with the increased demand. Consequently, developments in PPLV regarding resource management have gained huge importance in the modern world.
If we want to sustain technological innovation, and develop faster and smarter digital systems, research into the field of PPLV is a must.
