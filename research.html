UCL PPLV Research Area


The UCL Research Team is leading new developments into this field.
Particularly of note, Peter O'Hearn of the UCL PPLV research team developed Separation Logic which as has facilitated the development of programs to verify programs written in imperative programmming languages. Members of the UCL PPLV research team were also directly involved in developing Facebook's INFER program which implements separation logic, and is used to ensure that phone applications can be developed quickly.
