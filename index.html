What is PPLV?

PPLV is a Computer Science Research Area focused on investigating how rigorous mathematical models can be used in technical endeavors.
PPLV stands for Principles of Programming, Logic and Verification, and despite what it's name might suggest, it is not only important in high end computing but actually integral to forming the technology we use everyday.

Let's learn more together...

What does this site contain?

This website is aimed at increasing awareness for this field - to us, it seems that such an integral field should lack any proper awareness.
We hope to take you through an indepth tour of the area, from an intriguing exploration of PPLV's history, to highlighting any important contributors. We'd also like to indicate our very own University (UCL)'s contributions to this field, and finally wrap it up with an overview of PPLV's impact on the world.

So why does this site exist?

This site was made as part of UCL's Integrated Engineering Project - an attempt by the university to allow it's students to get a better understanding of the research that it does.
